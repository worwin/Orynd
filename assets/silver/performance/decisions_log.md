# Decisions Log

## Purpose
Track historical silver decisions, signals, and outcomes for evaluation and system improvement.

## Entry format
- Date
- Silver price at decision
- SLV price at decision
- Signal (bullish, bearish, neutral)
- Signal score (-2 to +2)
- Confidence (0-100%)
- Action taken
- Outcome (to be filled later)
- Notes

## Rules
- Append only
- Do not overwrite history
- Use consistent formatting
