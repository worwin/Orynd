# Current gold state

## Facts
- Real yields are elevated: US 10Y real yield (`DFII10`) 1.86% on 2026-03-18.
- Fed held rates at 3.50% to 3.75% on 2026-03-18; macro backdrop is not clearly easing.
- Gold is trading at 5,157.6, below the former 5,200 support area, with current support at 5,146.4 and resistance at 5,221.4.
- Geopolitical stress is elevated, with Iran-Israel conflict escalation reported on 2026-03-20.
- Gold ETF flows remained positive in February 2026 and central-bank demand was strong in 2025.
- Data timestamps are mixed, and the dollar proxy is a broad USD index rather than repo-preferred `DXY`.

## Interpretation
- Current regime is mixed.
- Macro is a headwind: elevated real yields and a steady Fed lean bearish for gold.
- Sentiment and structural demand are supportive: geopolitics, ETF inflows, and central-bank buying lean bullish.
- Market structure is cautious-to-weak in the short term because gold is below a former support zone near 5,200.
- Conservative read: do not force a directional view while macro and safe-haven signals conflict.

## Signal
- Regime: Neutral / conflicted
- Confidence: Medium

## Action
- Base case is hold and wait for cleaner alignment between macro and price.
- Do not treat current conditions as a high-conviction bullish re-entry.

## Invalidation
- This neutral regime view weakens if gold reclaims and holds above 5,200 while real yields soften.
- This neutral regime view also weakens if gold loses 5,146 support and macro remains restrictive, which would shift the balance more bearish.

## Top 3 facts driving conclusion
- Real yields remain elevated near 1.9%.
- Gold is below the former 5,200 support zone.
- Geopolitical stress and gold allocation demand remain supportive.

## Top 2 risks to thesis
- Geopolitical stress could override rates and trigger a sharp upside move despite restrictive macro.
- Mixed and slightly stale data, especially on the dollar side, could understate a recent macro turn.

## Key drivers to monitor
- Real yields
- USD (DXY)
- Geopolitical developments

## Regime
- Neutral / conflicted, conservative hold bias
