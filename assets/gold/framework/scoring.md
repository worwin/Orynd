# Scoring Framework

## Signal Score
- +2 = strong bullish
- +1 = mild bullish
- 0 = neutral
- -1 = mild bearish
- -2 = strong bearish

## Confidence Score
- 0–100%

## Interpretation
- Signal score reflects directional conviction
- Confidence reflects quality and consistency of inputs

## Rules
- Mixed signals → score near 0
- High confidence requires alignment across macro, sentiment, and price
