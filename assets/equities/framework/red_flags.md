# Red Flags

## Business
- Business model is too hard to explain simply
- Competitive position is weakening
- Customer concentration is high
- Regulatory or technological disruption risk is underappreciated

## Financial
- Leverage rising without clear payoff
- Margins deteriorating structurally
- ROE inflated by leverage rather than business strength
- Share dilution despite claimed shareholder alignment

## Management
- Incentives reward growth over value creation
- Repeated optimistic guidance resets
- Serial acquisitions obscure weak organic economics
