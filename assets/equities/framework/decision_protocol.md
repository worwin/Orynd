# Equities Decision Protocol

## Goal
Force a disciplined long-term equity decision that separates business facts from portfolio action.

## Required order
1. Facts
2. Interpretation
3. Quality
4. Valuation
5. Recommendation
6. Entry plan
7. Exit plan
8. Risks
9. Invalidation
10. Data freshness assessment

## Rules
- Facts must come from filings, company materials, or clearly cited market data.
- Interpretation may explain facts, but may not introduce uncited new facts.
- Technicals and sentiment are secondary to filings, quality, and valuation.
- Recommendation must be exactly one of BUY, HOLD, SELL, or INSUFFICIENT_DATA.
- Entry and exit prices must align with the valuation method and margin-of-safety rules.
- If filing coverage or accounting understanding is incomplete, confidence must be reduced.
- If the recommendation is BUY or SELL, the rationale must be explicit and decisive.
