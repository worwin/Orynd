# Buffett Rules

## What to prefer
- Understandable businesses
- Durable moats
- High returns on capital
- Consistent free cash flow
- Owner-oriented management
- Rational capital allocation
- Margin of safety

## What to avoid
- Complexity without clarity
- Weak or deteriorating moats
- Heavy dependence on adjusted earnings
- Serial dilution
- Value-destructive acquisitions
- Excess leverage without durable cash generation

## Margin of safety
- Standard target: `25-30%`
- Exceptional moat and sustained returns: `10-15%`
- Cyclical or accounting-heavy businesses: `35-40%`
