# Accounting Checks

## Required checks
- Revenue quality
- Cash conversion
- Stock-based compensation
- Share-count trend
- Working-capital swings
- One-time charges that keep repeating
- Acquisition accounting and goodwill
- Debt maturity and interest burden
- Maintenance versus growth capex

## Red flags
- Earnings growth without cash conversion
- Large recurring adjusted add-backs
- Buybacks offset only dilution
- FCF boosted by unsustainably favorable working capital
