# Valuation Rules

## Primary method
- Use Owners Earnings or DCF as the main intrinsic value method.

## Owners Earnings
- Approximation:
- `Net income + D&A - maintenance capex +/- change in working capital`
- Be conservative on maintenance capex and working capital needs.

## Scenario set
- Bear
- Base
- Bull

## Constraints
- Discount rate: use `max(10Y UST + 3%, 10%)` if a cleaner company-specific rate is not available.
- Terminal growth must stay conservative and below long-run nominal growth assumptions.
- Always sanity-check against normalized `P/E`, `EV/EBIT`, and `EV/FCF`.

## Decision mapping
- `BUY`: price at or below required margin of safety versus base IV
- `HOLD`: near fair value or high quality without enough discount
- `SELL`: clear overvaluation or thesis deterioration
