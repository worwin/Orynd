# Buffett Scoring Framework

## Buffett Quality Score
Total score: `0` to `100`

## Weights
- Moat: `25`
- Profitability and quality: `20`
- Financial strength: `15`
- Management and capital allocation: `15`
- Valuation and margin of safety: `25`

## Guidance
- `80-100`: exceptional
- `60-79`: solid
- `<60`: mediocre

## Rules
- A high quality score does not override a poor valuation.
- Valuation score must reflect discount or premium to intrinsic value, not just relative multiples.
- Penalize recurring add-backs, dilution, leverage creep, and unclear owner earnings.
- A recommendation near fair value should usually be `HOLD`, not `BUY`.
