# Position Sizing Rules

## Goal
Translate conviction and valuation into size.

## Suggested sizing bands
- `Starter`: incomplete conviction or small margin of safety
- `Core`: high quality and sufficient margin of safety
- `Max`: exceptional quality, strong confidence, and deep discount

## Rules
- Do not size a position aggressively if accounting quality is unclear.
- Do not use technical strength alone to justify size.
- Increase size only when both quality and valuation support it.
