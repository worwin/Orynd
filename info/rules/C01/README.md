# C01 - Two Great Revelations That Made Warren the Richest Person in the World

## Purpose
Start with two questions: does the business have a durable competitive advantage, and is the current price attractive enough to buy it.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses that can compound value for many years
- Only proceed when business quality and price both make sense
- Use later chapter tests as evidence for those two core questions

## No-Buy Signals
- Do not buy because a stock only looks statistically cheap
- Do not pay up for a business with an unclear moat
- Do not confuse price action with business quality

## AI Rules
- IF moat is unclear THEN reject or defer
- IF quality is high but price is unattractive THEN wait
- IF quality and valuation both work THEN continue
