# C19 - Net Earnings What Warren Is Looking For

## Purpose
Buffett wants a long-term upward trend in net earnings because consistency and growth together signal durable economics.

## Source
- PDF: `C19 - Net Earnings What Warren Is Looking For.pdf`
- OCR text: `C19 - Net Earnings What Warren Is Looking For.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer a multi-year upward trend in net earnings
- Treat consistency as more important than one strong year
- Use net income with buyback history and per-share trends

## No-Buy Signals
- Do not trust a single year of strong net income
- Avoid businesses whose net income swings wildly or trends down
- Avoid being fooled by EPS growth that comes only from shrinking share count

## AI Rules
- Require a historical upward trend in net earnings before a strong buy score
- IF net earnings are flat or falling THEN lower the durability score
- Cross-check net-income growth against share count and buybacks
