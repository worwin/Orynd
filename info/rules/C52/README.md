# C52 - Stock Buybacks Warren's Tax-Free Way to Increase Shareholder Wealth

## Purpose
Buybacks can be a tax-efficient way to enrich continuing shareholders when the company has real excess cash.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer companies that repurchase shares regularly from surplus cash flow
- Treat a sustained buyback history as evidence of strong underlying economics
- Use buybacks to explain rising per-share earnings and value per share

## No-Buy Signals
- Do not credit buybacks if they are offset by debt growth or weak operations
- Avoid confusing buyback-driven EPS growth with real business growth
- Avoid companies that issue shares and repurchase them without real net improvement

## AI Rules
- IF share count falls over time due to cash-funded buybacks THEN add positive capital-allocation evidence
- IF buybacks are debt-funded or mask weak earnings THEN reduce the score
- Explain EPS growth in terms of both net income and share count
