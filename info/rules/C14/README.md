# C14 - Depreciation A Cost Warren Can't Ignore

## Purpose
Depreciation is a real economic cost and a useful proxy for capital intensity.

## Source
- PDF: `C14 - Depreciation A Cost Warren Can't Ignore.pdf`
- OCR text: `C14 - Depreciation A Cost Warren Can't Ignore.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer low depreciation relative to gross profit
- Use a modest depreciation burden as a sign of easier economics
- Treat low capital intensity as supportive

## No-Buy Signals
- Do not use EBITDA to pretend depreciation does not matter
- Avoid businesses where depreciation takes a large slice of gross profit
- Avoid asset-heavy models whose accounting earnings overstate reality

## AI Rules
- IF depreciation_to_gross_profit is low and stable THEN score positively
- IF depreciation burden stays high THEN flag capital intensity
- Treat EBITDA-only reasoning as inconsistent with the Buffett method
