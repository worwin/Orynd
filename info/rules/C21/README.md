# C21 - Balance Sheet in General

## Purpose
The balance sheet reveals financial strength, capital intensity, and debt dependence, which help confirm whether the moat is real.

## Source
- PDF: `C21 - Balance Sheet in General.pdf`
- OCR text: `C21 - Balance Sheet in General.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Use the balance sheet to judge cash, liabilities, and funding needs
- Prefer businesses whose balance sheets reflect strength rather than survival financing
- Read it together with the other statements

## No-Buy Signals
- Do not assume a strong income statement is enough if the balance sheet is weak
- Avoid businesses that require constant outside capital
- Avoid conclusions from one balance-sheet line in isolation

## AI Rules
- Use the balance sheet as the second major confirmation layer
- IF debt or asset quality contradict the profit story THEN reduce confidence
- Require a coherent balance-sheet explanation before a high-conviction buy
