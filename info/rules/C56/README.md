# C56 - How Warren Determines the Right Time to Buy a Fantastic Business

## Purpose
For great businesses, timing is about paying the lowest sensible price, especially during bear markets or temporary one-time solvable problems.

## Buy Signals
- Buy more aggressively when a great business is available in a bear market
- Treat one-time solvable problems as potential entry points
- Remember that a lower price materially improves long-term returns

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid paying peak multiples during euphoric bull markets
- Avoid buying just because a business is wonderful if the price is too steep
- Avoid problems that are structural rather than solvable

## AI Rules
- IF a high-quality business is trading down because of a temporary solvable problem THEN raise buy interest
- IF the market is euphoric and the P/E is historically stretched THEN downgrade timing attractiveness
- Use price paid as a direct input into expected long-term return
