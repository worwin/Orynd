# C50 - The Cash Flow Statement Where Warren Goes to Find the Cash

## Purpose
The cash flow statement tells Buffett whether reported earnings are turning into real cash and how management is using that cash.

## Source
- PDF: `C50 - The Cash Flow Statement Where Warren Goes to Find the Cash.pdf`
- OCR text: `C50 - The Cash Flow Statement Where Warren Goes to Find the Cash.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses with healthy operating cash flow
- Use investing and financing cash flows to understand reinvestment and capital allocation
- Treat the cash flow statement as the reality check on accrual accounting

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses that report profit but chronically fail to generate cash
- Avoid ignoring financing inflows that make weak businesses look stronger
- Avoid reading net income without checking cash conversion

## AI Rules
- Cross-check net income against operating cash flow before approving a buy
- IF operating cash flow is weak relative to profits THEN flag earnings-quality risk
- Use investing and financing sections to explain where the cash really went
