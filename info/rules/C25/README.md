# C25 - Inventory What the Company Needs to Buy and What the Company Needs to Sell

## Purpose
In manufacturers, Buffett likes inventory growth that matches profitable demand, especially when products do not become obsolete quickly.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer manufacturers where inventory and net earnings rise together
- Favor products that stay relevant and do not obsolete quickly
- Treat orderly inventory growth as evidence of healthy demand

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid inventory spikes followed by collapses
- Avoid businesses where inventory becomes obsolete quickly
- Avoid treating inventory growth as positive when earnings do not follow

## AI Rules
- IF inventory growth is paired with net-earnings growth THEN score positively for manufacturers
- IF inventory builds while earnings weaken THEN flag risk
- Use different expectations for service businesses that naturally carry little inventory
