# C55 - More Ways to Value a Company with a Durable Competitive Advantage

## Purpose
Buffett-style valuation blends earnings yield, growth, discounting, and eventual market recognition of durable economics.

## Buy Signals
- Use long-term earnings growth to estimate future yield on cost and intrinsic value
- Compare price with discounted long-term earning power, not just today's multiple
- Value the business with an eye toward tax-deferred compounding

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid static one-year valuation frameworks for long-duration businesses
- Avoid buying when price already assumes unrealistic growth
- Avoid valuation work that ignores interest rates and alternative returns

## AI Rules
- Estimate normalized pretax earnings, growth, and discount rate before issuing a buy view
- IF market price is comfortably below durable long-term earning value THEN score positively
- Explain valuation in long-horizon compounding terms
