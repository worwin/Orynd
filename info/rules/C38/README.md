# C38 - Long-Term Debt Coming Due and the Troubles It Can Cause

## Purpose
Debt maturity schedules matter because even good businesses can be hurt if large obligations come due at the wrong time.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer companies that can meet upcoming maturities with earnings or cash
- Treat spread-out and manageable debt ladders as healthier than concentrated maturities
- Use maturities to test whether the business self-finances

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses facing large near-term debt walls without a clear plan
- Avoid depending on easy refinancing for the thesis to work
- Avoid companies whose maturity risk can erase decent economics

## AI Rules
- Inspect upcoming debt maturities before approving a buy
- IF maturities require refinancing under stress THEN reduce conviction
- Prefer businesses that could repay or refinance comfortably even in a downturn
