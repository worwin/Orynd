# C46 - Treasury Stock Warren Likes to See This on the Balance Sheet

## Purpose
Treasury stock often signals that a strong business is buying back its own shares with surplus cash.

## Buy Signals
- Treat the presence of treasury stock and a buyback history as positive evidence
- Prefer buybacks funded by strong business economics
- Use treasury stock as part of the capital-allocation picture

## No-Buy Signals
- Do not read treasury stock as automatically good without checking cash flow and debt
- Avoid overstating ROE when buybacks have artificially reduced equity
- Avoid buyback stories that coincide with weak core economics

## AI Rules
- IF treasury stock is present and the company has a sustained repurchase history THEN add positive evidence
- Adjust ROE interpretation for equity reduction caused by buybacks
- Use treasury stock with debt and cash-flow analysis before scoring strongly
