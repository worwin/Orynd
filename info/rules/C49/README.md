# C49 - The Problem with Leverage and the Tricks It Can Play on You

## Purpose
Leverage can make mediocre businesses look exceptional, so Buffett strips out debt-fueled illusions before trusting returns.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses whose earnings power comes from business economics rather than borrowed money
- Use leverage analysis to test whether high ROE is genuine
- Treat conservative balance sheets as part of the moat

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses that depend on large amounts of leverage
- Avoid mistaking debt-enhanced profitability for a durable advantage
- Avoid highly leveraged structures with fragile funding

## AI Rules
- IF strong returns are mainly leverage-driven THEN downgrade the business sharply
- Require leverage-adjusted reasoning before scoring ROE as a moat signal
- Favor businesses that earn well without balance-sheet tricks
