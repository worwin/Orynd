# C03 - Where Warren Starts His Search for the Exceptional Company

## Purpose
Warren starts with business models that naturally support superior economics: unique products, unique services, or low-cost operators.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer companies that own a place in the customer's mind
- Prefer institution-specific services rather than person-specific firms
- Prefer low-cost operators with a real scale advantage

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid people-dependent firms where talent captures the economics
- Avoid businesses with no clear product or service advantage
- Avoid companies that cannot explain repeat customer choice

## AI Rules
- IF company sells a unique product or service THEN add moat evidence
- IF company is the durable low-cost operator THEN add moat evidence
- IF economics depend on specific people rather than the institution THEN reduce moat score
