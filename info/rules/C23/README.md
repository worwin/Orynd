# C23 - Current Asset Cycle How the Money Is Made

## Purpose
Buffett wants to understand how cash moves through inventory and receivables so he can judge whether growth is attractive or burdensome.

## Source
- PDF: `C23 - Current Asset Cycle How the Money Is Made.pdf`
- OCR text: `C23 - Current Asset Cycle How the Money Is Made.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer working-capital cycles that turn into cash efficiently
- Look for models that do not need huge current-asset buildouts for growth
- Treat a smooth cycle as supportive evidence

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses whose growth endlessly ties up more cash
- Avoid working-capital cycles that look unstable or boom-bust driven
- Do not ignore working-capital strain just because earnings look okay

## AI Rules
- Map how cash becomes inventory and receivables before scoring growth quality
- IF growth requires disproportionate current-asset expansion THEN lower quality
- Use the current-asset cycle to understand mechanics, not as a standalone trigger
