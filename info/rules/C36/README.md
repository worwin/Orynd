# C36 - Accounts Payable, Accrued Expenses, and Other Current Liabilities

## Purpose
These line items usually reflect the day-to-day operating engine, so Buffett uses them mainly for context and discipline checks.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer operating liabilities that grow in line with a healthy business
- Treat normal payables and accruals as acceptable if the company remains cash generative
- Use them to understand working-capital discipline

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid unexplained surges in accruals or payables
- Avoid businesses that seem to finance themselves by stretching vendors
- Do not treat these liabilities as a direct moat signal

## AI Rules
- Use these categories as support metrics, not primary decision metrics
- IF they swell faster than the business without explanation THEN flag a quality review
- Cross-check with cash flow before drawing conclusions
