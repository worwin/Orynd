## Buy Signals
- Prefer gross margins of 40% or better over long periods
- Treat 50% to 60%+ margins as especially strong when consistent
- Review roughly ten years of history

## No-Buy Signals
- Treat 20% or below as a warning sign for fierce competition
- Avoid one-year margin spikes without consistency
- Do not ignore cases where other costs destroy the gross-margin advantage

## AI Rules
- IF gross_margin_10y_avg >= 40% THEN increase moat score
- IF gross_margin <= 20% THEN classify the industry as likely very competitive
- IF gross margins are high but later costs eat them away THEN downgrade the signal
