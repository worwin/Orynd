# C57 - How Warren Determines It Is Time to Sell

## Purpose
The default is to hold a wonderful business for a very long time, Buffett sells only when a better use of capital appears, the moat erodes, or valuation becomes extreme.

- PDF: `C57 - How Warren Determines It Is Time to Sell.pdf`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer holding rather than trading when the competitive advantage remains intact
- Sell when capital can move into a clearly better opportunity or the moat is weakening
- Treat extreme overvaluation as a valid reason to consider exiting

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid unnecessary selling that triggers taxes and interrupts compounding
- Avoid staying in a business after the competitive advantage is clearly deteriorating
- Avoid rolling proceeds from one bubble-priced stock into another bubble-priced stock

## AI Rules
- Set default_action = hold while moat remains durable and valuation is not extreme
- IF moat erosion is evident or a clearly better opportunity exists THEN allow sell consideration
- IF a super business trades around 40x earnings or more THEN flag possible extreme overvaluation and reassess
