# C54 - The Ever-Increasing Yield Created by the Durable Competitive Advantage

## Purpose
The real magic is that a durable advantage keeps growing earnings, so the investor's yield on original cost keeps rising.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses with long records of growing per-share earnings
- Treat rising yield on cost as evidence that the moat is converting into wealth
- Favor companies whose growth can continue without large new capital needs

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses whose yields look attractive only at purchase but do not grow
- Avoid temporary growth spurts that lack durability
- Avoid ignoring whether the moat is still intact

## AI Rules
- Project whether earnings growth can lift yield on cost over time
- IF the business can plausibly create a materially higher future yield on cost THEN add compounding evidence
- IF future yield improvement is doubtful THEN lower long-term attractiveness
