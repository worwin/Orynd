# C39 - Total Current Liabilities and the Current Ratio

## Purpose
Like Chapter 28, this chapter reinforces that classic liquidity ratios must be interpreted through Buffett's lens of earning power and durability.

## Buy Signals
- Allow low current ratios in genuinely strong cash-generating businesses
- Use current liabilities and current assets together rather than mechanically
- Judge whether the business can carry short-term obligations from ongoing economics

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid using the current ratio as a one-number decision rule
- Avoid weak businesses whose low current ratio reflects real fragility
- Do not confuse liquidity with business quality

## AI Rules
- Treat the current ratio as context, not a final verdict
- IF current liabilities are manageable because of strong earnings THEN do not auto-fail
- IF current liabilities are high and earnings are weak THEN downgrade aggressively
