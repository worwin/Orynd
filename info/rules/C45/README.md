# C45 - Retained Earnings Warren's Secret for Getting Superrich

## Purpose
Growing retained earnings are one of Buffett's favorite signs that the business is keeping profits and using them productively.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses that grow retained earnings over time
- Treat productive reinvestment of retained earnings as a major compounding engine
- Use retained-earnings growth as evidence that net worth is expanding

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid companies that fail to add to retained earnings over time unless the special case is clear
- Avoid businesses that retain earnings but do not convert them into higher future earnings
- Do not confuse special buyback-driven cases with ordinary weakness

## AI Rules
- IF retained_earnings trend upward over many years THEN score positively
- IF retained earnings are stagnant or falling and no strong special case exists THEN downgrade quality
- Judge retained earnings by what management earns on them, not just by their size
