# C26 - Net Receivables Money Owed to the Company

## Purpose
Receivables help Buffett judge whether reported sales are turning into collectible cash or just building accounting earnings.

## Source
- PDF: `C26 - Net Receivables Money Owed to the Company.pdf`
- OCR text: `C26 - Net Receivables Money Owed to the Company.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer receivables that grow in line with revenue and cash generation
- Treat disciplined collections as a positive sign
- Use receivables to verify that sales are real

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid companies where receivables rise much faster than sales
- Avoid businesses whose growth depends on hard-to-collect balances
- Do not ignore deteriorating cash conversion

## AI Rules
- Compare receivables growth with revenue growth and operating cash flow
- IF receivables materially outpace sales THEN flag earnings-quality issues
- Treat strong collections as support, not a standalone moat signal
