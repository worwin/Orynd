# C02 - The Kind of Business That Will Make Warren Superrich

## Purpose
The biggest wins come from durable businesses held for a long time, not from constant trading in cheap stocks.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer monopoly-like economics with low bankruptcy risk
- Allow fair prices for great businesses if long-term compounding is strong
- Favor setups where intrinsic value can keep rising for years

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid pure speculation and short-term trading setups
- Avoid cigar-butt bargains with real failure risk
- Avoid businesses that only work if market sentiment changes quickly

## AI Rules
- IF thesis depends on short-term re-rating THEN lower conviction
- IF earnings power can compound for years THEN add quality points
- IF solvency is weak THEN do not classify as Buffett-style
