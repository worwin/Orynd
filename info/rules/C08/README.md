# C08 - Revenue Where the Money Comes In

## Purpose
Revenue matters most when it is understandable, repeatable, and able to support good downstream economics.

## Source
- PDF: `C08 - Revenue Where the Money Comes In.pdf`
- OCR text: `C08 - Revenue Where the Money Comes In.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer stable or rising revenue from repeat demand
- Prefer pricing power over volume chasing
- Treat understandable revenue streams as higher quality

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid revenue growth that does not create durable profit
- Avoid highly erratic revenue
- Avoid businesses that must chase volume to survive

## AI Rules
- Score revenue positively when it is consistent and understandable
- If revenue rises while profitability weakens THEN flag it
- Do not use revenue growth alone as a buy signal
