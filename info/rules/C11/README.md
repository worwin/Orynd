# C11 - Operating Expenses Where Warren Keeps a Careful Eye

## Purpose
Strong gross margins only matter if operating expenses do not consume them.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses where operating expenses leave a healthy share of gross profit
- Review selling, research, depreciation, and interest together
- Look for stable expense discipline over time

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses whose gross advantage disappears after operating costs
- Avoid companies that need constant spending just to stay relevant
- Avoid expense patterns that swing wildly

## AI Rules
- Use operating expenses as the bridge between gross profit and true quality
- If recurring operating costs absorb most of gross profit THEN lower conviction
- Require consistency rather than one-time cuts
