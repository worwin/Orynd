## Buy Signals
- Prefer taxes paid that are consistent with reported pretax income
- Use taxes as a credibility check on earnings quality
- Treat straightforward tax accounting as a positive sign

## No-Buy Signals
- Be cautious when pretax income and taxes paid do not line up
- Avoid businesses whose headline profits look stronger than their tax reality suggests
- Do not ignore repeated tax anomalies without explanation

## AI Rules

- IF tax behavior materially conflicts with reported profitability THEN reduce confidence
