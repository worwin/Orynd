# C29 - Property, Plant, and Equipment For Warren Not Having Them Can Be a Good Thing

## Purpose
Large fixed-asset needs often weaken economics, so Buffett generally prefers businesses that do not require heavy plant and equipment.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses that can earn well without a large PP&E base
- Treat asset-light models as attractive when margins remain strong
- Use PP&E levels to infer future capex pressure

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses that must pour cash into factories and equipment
- Avoid equating physical scale with moat quality
- Avoid capital-intensive industries unless the economics are unusually compelling

## AI Rules
- IF PP&E needs are modest relative to earnings THEN add quality points
- IF the business requires heavy ongoing fixed-asset investment THEN flag capital intensity
- Use PP&E with depreciation and capex for a full reinvestment picture
