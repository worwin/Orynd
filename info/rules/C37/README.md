# C37 - Short-Term Debt How It Can Kill a Financial Institution

## Purpose
Short-term debt is dangerous because a business can be healthy on paper but still fail if near-term financing dries up.

- PDF: `C37 - Short-Term Debt How It Can Kill a Financial Institution.pdf`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses with little or no dependence on short-term debt
- Treat conservative short-term funding as especially important in financial firms
- Use low short-term debt as evidence of resilience

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid companies that rely heavily on rolling short-term obligations
- Avoid financial institutions whose funding model depends on constant market confidence
- Avoid buying into solvency risk disguised as liquidity pressure

## AI Rules
- IF short_term_debt is high relative to cash and earning power THEN downgrade sharply
- For financials weigh short-term funding risk heavily
- Prefer businesses that can meet obligations without continual refinancing
