# C15 - Interest Expense What Warren Doesn't Want

## Purpose
Low interest expense is one of Buffett's strongest signs that a business does not need much debt to function.

## Source
- PDF: `C15 - Interest Expense What Warren Doesn't Want.pdf`
- OCR text: `C15 - Interest Expense What Warren Doesn't Want.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer little or no interest burden
- In ordinary operating businesses, interest below 15% of operating income is attractive
- Within one industry, prefer the company with the lowest interest burden

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses that spend a large share of operating income on interest
- Be cautious when the ratio rises sharply over time
- Do not compare banks and industrials with the same standard

## AI Rules
- IF interest_expense_to_operating_income < 15% in a normal business THEN add strong positive evidence
- IF the ratio is high or rising THEN downgrade quality
