# C20 - Per-Share Earnings How Warren Tells the Winners from the Losers

## Purpose
Per-share earnings show what each remaining share is actually earning, which is crucial when buybacks or dilution change the share count.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer steadily rising per-share earnings over long periods
- Treat EPS growth backed by real net-income growth as high quality
- Look for buybacks that improve per-share economics without stressing the balance sheet

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid stagnant or declining per-share earnings
- Avoid EPS growth driven only by financial engineering
- Do not ignore dilution from persistent new share issuance

## AI Rules
- Track EPS over a long window
- IF EPS rises but net income does not THEN inspect buybacks, dilution, and leverage
- Use per-share results as the shareholder-level confirmation of quality
