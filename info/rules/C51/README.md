# C51 - Capital Expenditures Not Having Them Is One of the Secrets to Getting Rich

## Purpose
Low capital-expenditure needs are a powerful Buffett signal because they let more earnings stay with owners.

## Buy Signals
- Treat businesses using 50% or less of net earnings for capex as promising candidates
- Treat businesses using less than 25% of net earnings for capex as especially attractive
- Judge capex over roughly ten years, not one year

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses that consume most of their earnings in capital expenditures
- Avoid capital-hungry industries that require debt just to keep operating
- Avoid businesses whose capex burden destroys future compounding

## AI Rules
- IF ten_year_capex_to_net_income <= 50% THEN add positive evidence
- IF ten_year_capex_to_net_income < 25% THEN add strong positive evidence
- IF capex regularly exceeds earnings THEN classify the business as lower quality
