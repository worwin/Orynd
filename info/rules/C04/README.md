# C04 - Durability Is Warren's Ticket to Riches

## Purpose
Durability matters because stable products and stable economics reduce reinvestment needs and support long-term compounding.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer products or services that can stay relevant for decades
- Look for consistency in margins, debt, and earnings
- Treat multi-year stability as evidence of durability

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses that must constantly reinvent themselves
- Avoid companies with erratic financial patterns
- Avoid stories that do not show up in the statements

## AI Rules
- IF the last decade shows consistency across key metrics THEN add durability score
- IF product cycles drive constant reinvention THEN penalize durability
- IF consistency is missing THEN do not label the moat durable
