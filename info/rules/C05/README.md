## Buy Signals
- Use the income statement to judge margins and earnings direction
- Use the balance sheet to judge cash, debt, and capital needs
- Use the cash flow statement to judge reinvestment burden and buybacks

## No-Buy Signals
- Do not rely on only one statement
- Do not ignore cash generation when profits look good
- Do not use a single quarter as proof

## AI Rules
- Require cross-checks across all three statements
- If one statement conflicts with the others THEN lower confidence
