# C16 - Gain (or Loss) on Sale of Assets and Other

## Purpose
One-time gains or losses on asset sales are not the core earnings power Buffett wants to own.

## Source
- PDF: `C16 - Gain (or Loss) on Sale of Assets and Other.pdf`
- OCR text: `C16 - Gain (or Loss) on Sale of Assets and Other.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Focus on recurring operating earnings rather than incidental gains
- Treat asset-sale gains as noncore unless clearly repeatable
- Use this line to clean up normalized earnings

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses that need asset sales to appear profitable
- Do not capitalize one-time gains
- Do not ignore recurring losses hidden in other buckets

## AI Rules
- Remove one-time gains and losses when estimating normalized earnings
- IF reported profit depends materially on asset sales THEN classify earnings quality as weak
- Base buy decisions on operating economics
