# C53 - Warren's Revolutionary Idea of the Equity Bond and How It Has Made Him Superrich

## Purpose
Buffett can view a great stock as an equity bond where pretax earnings are the coupon and the coupon grows over time.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses with predictable pretax earnings and a durable moat
- Buy when the initial pretax yield on the purchase price is attractive relative to alternatives
- Favor businesses whose earnings can grow and lift yield on cost

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses whose future pretax earnings are too uncertain to model
- Avoid using the equity-bond framework on weak or cyclical businesses without durability
- Avoid focusing only on current price multiples without the earnings path

## AI Rules
- Model pretax_earnings / purchase_price as the starting equity-bond yield
- IF the yield is attractive and likely to grow THEN score positively
- IF earnings predictability is weak THEN do not use the equity-bond framing
