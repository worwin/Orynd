# C13 - Research and Development Why Warren Stays Away from It

## Purpose
Heavy ongoing R&D often signals a moat that is less durable because the business must keep reinventing itself.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses that do not require heavy R&D just to stay competitive
- Favor stable products and services with little redesign risk
- Treat low or no R&D burden as a strong sign of durability

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses whose edge depends on patents or rapid product cycles
- Avoid industries where high R&D is a permanent toll
- Avoid assuming today's tech leader is automatically durable

## AI Rules
- IF R&D spending is structurally high THEN reduce durability confidence
- IF economics remain strong without large R&D spend THEN add moat evidence
- Explain that heavy R&D may work operationally but is not the Buffett ideal
