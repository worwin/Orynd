# C32 - Long-Term Investments One of the Secrets to Warren's Success

## Purpose
Long-term investments can be powerful when management uses excess cash intelligently and patiently.

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses that can redeploy surplus cash into high-return long-term investments
- Treat disciplined capital allocation as a moat enhancer
- View long-term investments favorably when the core business already throws off excess cash

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid empire-building or unrelated investments
- Avoid assuming every investment portfolio is a plus
- Avoid management teams that hoard cash without productive deployment

## AI Rules
- IF long-term investments are paired with strong core economics and rational capital allocation THEN add positive evidence
- IF they reflect unfocused diversification THEN downgrade the signal
- Judge long-term investments by returns and discipline, not size alone
