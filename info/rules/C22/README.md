# C22 - Assets

## Purpose
Assets matter less as raw size and more for what they imply about capital needs and business economics.

## Source
- PDF: `C22 - Assets.pdf`
- OCR text: `C22 - Assets.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer businesses that can earn well without huge assets
- Treat asset-light economics as a positive when profitability remains strong
- Use asset composition to understand the business model

## No-Buy Signals
- Do not assume more assets mean a better business
- Avoid asset-heavy businesses that need continual reinvestment
- Avoid structures where assets are large but returns are poor

## AI Rules
- Use asset structure to infer capital intensity
- IF a company needs a large asset base for mediocre returns THEN downgrade quality
- Favor asset efficiency over asset size
