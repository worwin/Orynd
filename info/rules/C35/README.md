# C35 - Current Liabilities

## Purpose
Current liabilities matter for liquidity, but Buffett reads them in the context of the whole business rather than as an isolated red flag.

## Source
- PDF: `C35 - Current Liabilities.pdf`
- OCR text: `C35 - Current Liabilities.txt`

## Buy Signals
- Prefer current obligations that are easily covered by cash and earning power
- Use current liabilities to understand near-term pressure
- Treat manageable short-term obligations as normal

## No-Buy Signals
- Avoid businesses whose current liabilities overwhelm operating strength
- Do not treat current liabilities alone as a moat test
- Avoid ignoring refinancing or liquidity pressure in weak companies

## AI Rules
- Read current liabilities together with cash, current assets, and operating cash flow
- IF near-term obligations are high and earning power is weak THEN lower the score
- Do not auto-fail a strong business for routine current liabilities
